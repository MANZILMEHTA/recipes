HOMEMADE CHOCOLATE CAKE

The process of making chocolate cake at home is very simple & it's taste will be as same
as market cake and that too without an oven. So let's begin!





  • Preparation time=60 minutes
  • Baking time=60 minutes
  • Decoration time=30 minutes

    INGREDIENTS AND PREPARATORY FOR CAKE-

    > Milk - 1 cup(200ml)
    > Powdered sugar - 1 cup(130 gms)
    > Oil - 1/2 cup
    > Vinegar - 1 tablespoon
    > Vanilla essence - 1/2 teaspoon
    > All purpose flour - 1 and 1/2 cup(approx. 200gms)
    > Cocoa powder - 2 tablespoon
    > Baking powder - 1 teaspoon
    > Baking soda - 1/2 teaspoon
    > Oven tin(6-7)inches
    > sugar syrup - 9 tablespoon

    INGREDIENTS FOR CHOCOLATE GANACHE-

    > Chopped dark chocolate - 250 gms
    > Fresh cream - 150gms
    > Butter - 1/2 teaspoon

    METHODS/STEPS-

    1. In a bowl, add milk (room temperature), powdered sugar, oil, vinegar, vanilla essence and mix it well.

    2. Keep aside for 5 minutes . after 5 minutes, mix well.

    3. On that same bowl, keep the strainer and add all purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and strain it.

    4. Now mix well until no lumps, add 2 tablespoon of milk in it (for adjusting consistency).

    5. Now cake better is ready. Grease the oven tin with butter, put baking paper, pour the batter in the tin and tap the tin.

    6. Take the pan, put stand or small bowl to increase the height and cover it with a lid. Preheat the pan for 10 minutes on medium flame.

    7. After 10 minutes put the cake tin inside the pan and close the lid.

    8. Bake it for 50-55 minutes on low flame
      OR
      For oven, preheat at 200 degree celsius for 10 minutes and bake it at 180 degree celsius for 35-40 minutes.

    9. After 55 minutes check the cake with knife. If knife comes out clean, switch off the flame or else again keep it for 5 minutes.


    10. After cooling the cake, demould the cake. You can see that the cake is super spongy.


    11. For preparing chocolate ganache, take chocolate in a bowl and mix the fresh cream and butter.Mix it well and leave it for 5 minutes.

    12. After 5 minutes stir well. Now keep it in the fridge for 10 minutes to set.

    13. Now trim the spongy cake from top and cut the cake in three layers.

    14. After the ganache cools, whisk it for 1 minute.

    15. Take the base layer, spread 3 tablesppon of sugar syrup on it and spread the ganache.

    16. Repeat the above step for the remaining layers of cake, cover the whole cake with ganache and keep it in fridge for 20-25 minutes.

    17. After 25 minutes, ganache sets properly. For dripping, add 2 tablespoon of milk in leftover ganache and heat up in double boiler.

    18. Fill it in the piping bag and drip it on the cake. Keep it in fridge for 15 minutes.

    19. After it sets, make design of your choice.
           

    And the chocolate cake is ready! Now it's time for you to go for it.
