HOMEMADE CHOCOLATE CAKE

The process of making chocolate cake at home is very simple & it's taste will be same
as market cake and without oven.So let's begin!





  • Preparation time=60 minutes
  • Baking time=60 minutes
  • Decortion time=30 minutes

    INGREDIENTS AND PREPARATORY FOR CAKE-

    -> Milk - 1 cup
    -> powdered sugar - 1 cup
    -> Oil - 1/2 cuo
    -> Vinegar - 1 tbsp
    -> Vanilla essence - 1/2 tsp
    -> All purpose flour - 1 and 1/2 cup
    -> Cocoa powder - 2 tbls
    -> Baking powder - 1 tsp
    -> Baking soda - 1/2 tsp
    -> Oven tin(6-7)inchs

    INGREDIENTS FOR CHOCOLATE GANACHE-

    -> Chopped dark chocolate - 250 gms (2 cups)
    -> Fresh cream - 150 gms (1 cup)
    -> Butter - 1/2 tsp

    METHODS/STEPS-

    1. In a bowl add milk (room temperature),powdered sugar ,oil , vinegar, vanilla essence and mix it well.

    2. Keep aside for 5 minutes , after 5 minutes mix well.

    3. In that bowl keep the strainer and add all purpose flour,cocoa powder,baking powder,baking soda and starin it.

    4. Now mix well until no lumps,add 2tbls of milk (for adjusting consistency).

    5. Now cake better is ready ,Now grease the oven tin (6-7 inch tin),put baking paper,pour the better in tin and tap the tin.

    6. Preheat the pan for 10 minuteson medium flame,after 10 minutes put the cake tin inside the pan and close the lid.

    7. Bake it for 50-55 minutes on low flame
      OR
      bake in preheated oven at 180 degree celsius for 35-40 minutes

    8. After 55 minutes check the cake with knief if knief comes out clean swtich off the flame or again keep it for 5 minutes.


    9. After cooling the cake ,demould the cake.You can see that the cake is super spongy.


    10. For preparing chocolate ganache take chocolatein a boel n mix the fresh cream and butter and mix it well ,leave it for 5 minutes.

    11. 5 minutes stir well , now keep it in the fridge for 10 minutes to set.

    12. Now trim the spongy cake from top and cut the cake in three layers.after the ganache cools,whisk it for 1 minute.

    13. Assemble the cake,spread sugar syrup well on the 1st slice from the bottom,and spead the ganache.

    14. Repeat the above step for the remaining slices of cake,cover the whole cake wih the ganache and keep it in fridge for 20-25 minutes.

    15. After 25 minutes set completely, For dripping add 2tbsp milk in leftover ganache and heat up in double boiler.

    16. Fill it in the piping bag,drip it on the cake.keep it in the fridge for 15 minutes.

    17. After set make the design of your own.
           

    And the chocolate cake is ready! Now it's time for you to go for it.
