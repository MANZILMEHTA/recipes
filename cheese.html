HOMEMADE CHEESE

The process of making cheese at home is very simple and it's taste will be same as
mozzarella cheese which we get in the market. So let's begin!





  • Preparation time=60 minutes
  • Refrigeration time=3 hours

    INGREDIENTS AND PREPARATORY FOR CHEESE-

    -> Raw milk -500ml
    -> Salt -5gms
    -> Vinegar - 4 table spoon (60 ml)
    -> Boiling water - 6 cups (1200 ml)
    -> Ice cold water - 6 cups (1200 ml)
    -> Plastic wrap

    METHODS/STEPS-

    1. Take amul 500 ml milk ,SRICTLY RAW MILK, in a vessel. Add 5 gms salt and keep it on low flame, you have to boil it just for 2 mins or till it is lukewarm.


    2. After switching off the flame, add vinegar (60 ml) and keep stirring it for 10 minutes.


    3. Now you can see that milk is curdled. So now take a bowl, keep strainer on it and keep a thin cloth in strainer.


    4. Now strain the curdled milk.Then squeeze the cloth and remove excess of water from it.


    5. Now take two bowls. The first bowl is filled with 3 cups (600 ml) of boiling hot water and the second bowl is filled 3 cups (600 ml) of ice cold water.


    6. The curd which is left in the cloth will be in shape of a ball. Pick the ball of curd lightly and carefully.Then dip it in hot boiling water bowl for 2 minutes and meanwhile keep stirring it lightly with the help of spoon, ensuring that BALL DOES NOT BREAK.


    7. Now after 2 minutes,Remove the ball and put it again in cloth and squeeze it to remove water. transfer that ball into ice cold chilled water bowl for 2 minutes and again stir it lightly ensuring that ball does not break.
      Take the ball out and put it in between your palms of both hands. Squeeze the ball and start removing water from it.


    8. Once you feel that there is no water inside it ,again put the ball in hot water bowl, remove the ball, put it in cloth and squeeze it to remove water. Then put it in cold water and squeeze it between your palms AS BEFORE.


    9. Repeat this process for 8 to 10 times. Ensure that there is no water in the ball. Now you will observe that it has converted into an Elastic Cheese.


    10. Once you are done with this process wrap your cheese into a plastic wrap tightly such that no air should be there inside the wrap.


    11. Now keep this wrapped cheese in refrigerator for minimum 3 hours.


    12. So now your cheese is ready!! You can grate your cheese and see how elastic it is, similar to market cheese.


      13. And the cheese is ready! Now it's time for you to go for it.
