HOMEMADE CHEESE

The process of making cheese at home is very simple & it's taste will be same as
mozzarella cheese which we get in the market. So let's begin!





  • Preparation time=60 minutes
  • Refrigeration time=3 hours

    INGREDIENTS FOR CHEESE-

    -> Raw milk -500ml -> Salt -5gms -> vinegar ->4 tbl spoon (60ml)

    METHODS/STEPS-

    1. Take the amul 500 ml milk strickly raw milk in a vessel and keep it on low flame,
      u have to boil is for just 5 mins not more.
    2. After switching off the flame add vinegar and keep stirring it for 10 minutes.
    3. Now you can see that milk is curdled ,so now take a bowl keep strainer on it and
      keep a thin cloth in strainer.
    4. Now starin the curdled milk and by holding that cloth in your hand start
      removing excess of water from it.
    5. Now take two bowls half filled with water in one bowl fill full hot water and
      in other bowl fill chilled ice water.
    6. So now thw curd which is ther in the cloth remove it n deep it in hot water bowl
      for 2 minutes and keep stirring it slightly with the help of spoon.
    7. now after 2 minutes transfer it into chilled water bowl for 2 mins and again stir it
      and now with the help of your hands start removing water from it.
    8. Once you feel that now there is no water inside it ,again put that in hot water bowl
      and then in clod water.
    9. repeate that process for 8 to 10 times,and now you will observe that it is converting
      into an elastic Cheese.
    10. Once you are done with this process wrap your cheese into a plastic tightly and
      no air should ne there inside the wrap.
    11. now keep this cheese in Refrigerator for minimum 3 hours.
    12. So your cheese is ready!! now you can grate your cheese and see how elastic it is,
      similar to market cheese.

      13. And the cheese is ready! Now it's time for you to go for it.
