PIZZA WITH HOMEMADE PIZZA BASE

The process of making pizza base at home is very simple & it's taste will be same as DOMINOES
and PIZZA HUT's pizzas. So let's begin!





  • Preparation time=60 minutes
  • Baking time=25 minutes

    • INGREDIENTS FOR PIZZA BASE-

    -> All purpose flour - 250gms
    -> Dry yeast - 10gms
    -> Sugar - 10gms
    -> Salt - 5gms
    -> Water - 180ml
    -> Oil or butter or ghee - 10gms

    METHODS/STEPS-

    1. Mix Water,Yeast,Sugar In A Bowl And Cover It With A Lid, Keep Aside For 10 mins.(Till yeast is totally ready you can see the bubbles)

    2. Mix flour and salt in a seperate bowl and add yeast mixture into it, knead a soft dough with the help of electric beater and if you dont have that you can mix it with wisk or fork.
      Mix it for around 14-15 minutes.

    3. Mix oil or butter or ghee and again knead a little for about 8-10 minutes.

    4. After kneading it transfer the dough into a bowl and apply some oil or butter on the surface of dough and cover it with a lid.
      Keep it for around 40-45 minutes till it becomes double the size of original dough.

    5. Now pre-heat your oven at 200 degree celsius for 10 minutes.

    6. Now divide the dough into 2 portion, take one portion and put it in pizza tray and with the help of some flour,spread the dough in the tray with hand.

    7. Now apply pizza sauce of your choice[Schezwan sauce, Mayonnaise, Ketchup, Chilli sauce(optional)]

    8. Put vegetables of our choice(Tomato, Corn, Bell pepper, Olives and many more).

    9. Now sprinkle herbs(oregano, red chilli flakes, pizza seasoning).

    10. Now put cheese (you can use any cheese you want).
      For Homemade Cheese Recipe click here >> click here!


    11. Now put tray in oven and bake at 180 degree celsius for 20-22 minutes.
           

    And the pizza is ready! Now it's time for you to go for it.
