PIZZA WITH HOMEMADE PIZZA BASE

It is very easy to make pizza base and the taste will be same as DOMINOSE or PIZZA-HUT pizza's so let's start making it.



preparation time=60 minutes
baking time=25 minutes

INGREDIENTS FOR PIZZA BASE

  • All purpose flour - 250gms
  • dry yeast - 10gms
  • sugar - 10gms
  • salt - 5gms
  • water - 180ml
  • oil or butter or ghee - 10gms

METHODS/STEPS

  1. Mix Water,Yeast,Sugar In A Bowl And Cover It With A Lid, Keep Aside For 10 mins.(till yeast is totally activate you can see the bubbles)
  2. Mix flour and salt,knead a soft dough with the help of electric beater and if you dont have that you can mix it with wisk or fork.
    mix it for around 14-15 minutes.
  3. Mix oil or butter or ghee and again knead a little about 8-10 minutes
  4. after kneading it transfer the dough into a bowl and apply some oil or butter on the surface of it and cover it with a lid.
    keep it around 40-45 minutes till it becomes double the size of original doungh.
  5. now pre-heat youe oven at 200 degree celsius for 10 minutes
  6. now divide the dough into 2 portion, take one portion and put it in pizza tray and with the help of some flour with hand spread the dough in the tray
  7. now apply your pizza sauce of your choice you can apply(schezwan sauce,mayonnaise,ketchup,chilli sauce(optional))
  8. put vegetables of our choice(tomato,corn,bell pepper,olives and many more)
  9. now sprinkle herbs(oregano,red chilli flakes,pizza seasoning)
  10. now put cheese (you can use any cheese you want)
    For Homemade Cheese Recipe click here!


  11. now put tray in oven and bake at 180 degree celsius for 20-22 minutes
!!And your PIZZA is ready!!
